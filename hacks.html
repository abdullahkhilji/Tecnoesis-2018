FAQ

FREQUENTLY
ASKED QUESTIONS

Q. What is a hackathon?

Ans. Hackathon, also known as the HACK DAY, HACKFEST or CODEFEST, are renowned events where in people holding expertise in different fields from computer programming to graphic designing work in harmony on software projects.

Q. Are there any travel reimbursements in NITS::HACKS 2.0?

Unfortunately, we are not able to provide travel reimbursements to participants. But we’re always on the hunt to find more sustainable ways to bring more students to the event.

Q. Will I annoy people if I ask them questions?

Not at all! This is a common misconception about hackathons or coding competition and in actual fact, it’s the complete opposite. The environment at these events are designed specifically to encourage everyone to have fun and help each other. If you’re ever stuck on anything, go find a volunteer who might able to help and if not, they will almost certainly know who can.

Q. Will there be food?

Yes, Of course!!! The only thing that you should be focused on during the competition is to materialize your ideas and build great solutions. We will make sure you don't stay hungry.

HOW TO
REACH

National Institute of Technology, Silchar is located just on the outskirts of the city of Silchar and is well connected to other parts of the country by a good network of road, rail or air. The distance of Silchar from the economic hub of the state, Guwahati is only 326 km. The major ways to reach us:

AIR: There are daily direct flights to Silchar from cities like Delhi, Bangalore, Kolkata, Guwahati and Imphal. Air India, Spice Jet and Jet Airways operate flights from Silchar to Delhi, Bangalore, Kolkata, Guwahati and Imphal. From airport, the Institute campus is approximately 35 Kms. Hired private taxi service is available from the airport. The Training and Placement Cell helps recruiters reach the campus from the Airport.

RAIL: Silchar has a direct rail link with Kolkata, Delhi, Guwahati. The campus is around 10 KMs from the Silchar railway station. The Training and Placement Cell helps recruiters reach the campus from the railway Station.

ROAD: Silchar is well connected with various cities through road. A 326 km long National Highway directly connects Silchar with Guwahati. A large number of day/night bus ply between Guwahati and Silchar in the morning and evening everyday. After you arrive at ISBT-Silchar. The Training and Placement Cell helps recruiters reach the campus.
